About me:

Aerial photos and videos captured, spruced up and delivered to you quickly. I understand that in the world of real estate today, homes sell almost as quickly as they are listed. That is why I am committed to getting you final shots and cuts of your property within a day of the shoot. That said, I am an FAA certified drone pilot that will take the time to make sure you are completely satisfied with how good I make your property or project look. If you don't like how your photos or video came out, I will work hard to make it right.

I operate out of the Hillsborough, New Hampshire area.
