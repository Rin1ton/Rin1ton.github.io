Malika

Group Size: 2
Language: C#
My Contribution: All the programming!
Work Description:
Malika was my wife's capstone project for Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, New York. The final grade was a 98 for the project. I did all the programming for the project, so player movement, the primary telekineses ability, enemy behavior, level logic, I worked on it all. I made the backgound parallax behind the player as they walked by checking a distance value for each layer and making them move in the opposite direction to the player at different speeds.
